One more technicality

The pixel coordinate is based on the canvas size which is a bit unhandy for multiple reasons:

  • You do not want to rewrite the shader for every screen / canvas size
  • Keeping all values normalized (between 0.0 and 1.0) allows you to combine values more easily and express relationships
  • To normalize our coordinates we just have to divide them by our canvas size (= resolution)!
